Compliance

Key Compliance Areas We Cover

FEMA & FDI Compliance

Foreign Exchange Management Act requirements and Foreign Direct Investment approvals. We handle the complete compliance framework for your India entry.

GST Registration & Filing

Goods and Services Tax registration, ongoing monthly/quarterly filing, and input tax credit management for businesses operating in India.

Company Incorporation

Private Limited, LLP, OPC incorporation. DIN, DSC, MOA, AOA — complete end-to-end incorporation with MCA. Typically 10–15 working days.

Labor Law Compliance

EPF, ESI, shop establishment, and state-specific labor compliance across all Indian states. Essential for any business with employees in India.

SEZ Benefits

Special Economic Zone tax exemptions and benefits for eligible manufacturing and service businesses. We assess eligibility and manage applications.

Import Compliance

Customs regulations, import duties, GST on imports, IGST input credit, and all documentation for importing goods into India.
